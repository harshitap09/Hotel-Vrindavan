A Sacred Refuge
in Vrindavan

Nestled close to the holy temples of Vrindavan, Hotel Vrindavan is a modest, warm retreat for pilgrims and travellers seeking peace after a day of darshan. We offer clean, comfortable rooms with heartfelt hospitality — no extravagance, just genuine care and serenity.

The Experience

More Than a Stay

At Hotel Vrindavan, we believe your journey doesn't end at the temple door. We help make every moment of your visit sacred and serene.

🪈
Devotional Ambience

Soft bhajan music, floral decor, and a calm atmosphere that honours the spirit of Vrindavan.

🦚
Pilgrimage Guidance

Our staff can help plan temple visits, parikrama timings, and local dham routes.

🌿
Pure Vegetarian

Only sattvic, freshly cooked meals — prepared with love, no onion or garlic.

Warm Hospitality

A small hotel means personal attention. We treat every guest like family.
