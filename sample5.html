The story

The Lore

When we first walked into this abandoned lakeside inn after a wrong turn on a rainy Tuesday, opening a hotel was the last thing on our minds. We were three weary travelers looking for shelter. But the old stone walls whispered something — a quiet magic, a hush that felt like home. So we stayed. Restored every creaky floorboard, saved every vintage key. Now we invite you to stay, too.
