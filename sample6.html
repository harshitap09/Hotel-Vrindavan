The story

The Lore

When we first stumbled into this forgotten lakeside inn after a wrong turn on a rainy Tuesday, opening a hotel was the last thing on our minds. We were three weary travelers looking for shelter. But the old stone walls and the rusty orange door whispered something — a quiet magic, a hush that felt like home. So we stayed. Restored every floorboard, painted every shutter with berry reds and mustard yellows. Now we invite you to soak in the color, too.
